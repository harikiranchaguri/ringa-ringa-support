Overview

Ringa Ringa helps users create custom iPhone ringtones from microphone recordings and imported audio files.

Data Collection

Ringa Ringa does not collect personal data for tracking or advertising.

Audio files are processed on your device and saved locally unless you choose to share/export them.

Permissions

The app requests microphone permission only when you choose to record audio.
